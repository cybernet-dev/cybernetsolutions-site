# Momentum Help

## Setup

Tap + to add an Area, name the area, and write one small daily commitment. Also its important to describe why this is important to you to help you stay motivated.

## Commitments

Good commitments are specific and easy to repeat.

For example:

- Health: Walk 20 minutes
- Learning: Read 10 pages
- Relationships: Text someone I care about

## Daily Flow

The Current Focus is the highest-priority incomplete area. You can complete these in any order but it helps if you prioritize them. Tap a card to mark it complete. Tap it again if you need to undo.

## Manage Areas

Swipe a card to edit or delete it. Use 'Menu -> Reorder Areas' to prioritize your Area cards.

## Progress

As you complete your commitments your progress is shown in 'Today's Progress'. Recent History show your progress over the last seven days. For additional history select 'Menu -> History'.

## Reminders

From the 'Menu -> Settings' you can control morning and evening reminders. 'Reset' clears areas and completion history.

## Privacy

All User Data is local to this device. No data is sent to a server. We DO NOT collect any data from your device.
